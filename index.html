How do you use it?

  1. 1. To start, click on "Start a New List"
  2. 2. Click on "Enter Tasks Here", type your task, then click on the Add button or hit Enter to add it to the list.
  3. 3. You can cross off completed tasks by simply clicking on them.
  4. 4. You can remove items by clikcking on the "Delete" button.
